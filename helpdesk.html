what is biome?

biome is a web project that focuses on roleplay, creation, and collaboration. it's a world with its own lore, locations, mechanics, and creatures, which are all realized by its contributors.

my goal with biome is to create an ecosystem of materials and creators who work with each other to populate this world. i want to encourage interactivity and participation.

what can i submit?

there are no public projects currently.

how do i get on the phone book?

the phone book is for resident creators. i made it as a way to credit frequent contributors of biome. if you'd like to work more closely with what's going on here, please feel free to reach out at mailbox@biome.zip. otherwise, contributors are usually credited on individual project pages.
